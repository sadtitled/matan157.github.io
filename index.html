About

A Computer Science major attending CUNY Brooklyn College, In my free time I design and develop websites for friends and family. As a sophomore at Brooklyn College, I decided to take Intro to C++ with no programming/coding experience. That's when I found my passion. A year after I began programming, I have taught myself: Java, C#, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, and Bootstrap 3—all in my free time. I continue creating, learning, and growing. Currently I'm working on developing web applications and which technologies to utilize.

Apart from developing, I am a musician. Whether it's on trombone or guitar, I have played fourteen different broadway shows with several different theatres. Some of these include Fiddler on the Roof, Les Miserables, Hairspray, and Crazy for You. When I'm not in the pit, I'm on the stage playing with Eli Whitney and the Sound Machine.
